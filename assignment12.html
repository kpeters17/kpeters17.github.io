Assignment 12

This lesson was about incorporating accessibility into websites. I learned about how important it is to have accessibility so everyone can be included. It is very unfair for someone to be unable to access content on a website because they have a visual, audio or cognitive impairment. I learned how to use the WAVE report to analyze a web page and gain insight into things I can do to make my sites more accessible to them. This was an invaluable lesson and something I will make part of my standard procedure in creating web pages for a product or service. By making sure everyone has an opportunity to access content I will increase web traffic to my pages and make sure no one feels excluded. I will make sure anyone who is disabled will have full access to every suggestion that the WAVE report makes.
